About Us

Ocean City's Liquor Destination

Gold Coast Beer Wine & Liquor is a Class A 7-Day licensed package store on Coastal Highway in Ocean City, Maryland. We serve locals, vacationers, and everyone in between with one of the largest selections on the Eastern Shore.

Whether you're stocking up for a beach week, grabbing a six-pack for the evening, or hunting for a specific bottle — we've got you covered.
