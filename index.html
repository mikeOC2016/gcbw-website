About Gold Coast

Gold Coast Beer Wine & Liquor is a Class A 7-Day licensed package store located on Coastal Highway in Ocean City, Maryland. We serve locals, vacationers, and everyone in between with one of the largest selections on the Eastern Shore.

Whether you're stocking up for a beach week, grabbing a six-pack for the evening, or hunting for a specific bottle — we've got you covered. Our knowledgeable staff is always happy to help you find exactly what you need.
